How to apply in Gmail:

  1. Click Copy Signature above.
  2. Navigate to your Gmail Settings (Gear icon → See all settings).
  3. On the General tab, scroll down to Signature.
  4. Select/create a signature, click inside the box, and press Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac).
  5. Scroll all the way down and save changes!
